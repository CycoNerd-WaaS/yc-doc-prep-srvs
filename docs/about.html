About Document Assistance

Meet Yaneth Cisneros

Owner and operator of Document Assistance, serving the Pomona Valley community for over three decades.

With more than 30 years of experience in tax preparation and document assistance, Yaneth Cisneros has built a reputation for accuracy, professionalism, and compassionate service. As a CTEC Registered Tax Preparer and California Notary Public, she provides reliable administrative support to individuals and families throughout the Pomona Valley.

Document Assistance is conveniently located in downtown Pomona, offering professional services with the personal attention of a neighborhood business. Whether you need help with your annual tax return, require notarization of important documents, or need assistance preparing court forms, we are here to help — at your specific direction.
