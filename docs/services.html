Notary Public

Commission #2458791

As a California commissioned Notary Public, Yaneth provides official notarization services for a variety of legal documents. A notary serves as an impartial witness to the signing of important documents.

Services Include:

  • Acknowledgments
  • Jurats (Oaths)
  • Certified Copies of Powers of Attorney
  • Affidavits
  • Deeds & Contracts
  • Powers of Attorney
