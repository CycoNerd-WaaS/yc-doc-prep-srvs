Tax Preparation

CTEC (California Tax Education Council) registers and regulates tax preparers in California. Using a CTEC-registered preparer ensures they've completed required education and passed background checks. Our CTEC ID is verifiable at ctec.org.

Bring your W-2s, 1099s, Social Security cards for all family members, photo ID, last year's tax return, and any other income or deduction documents. We'll let you know if we need anything else.

Legal Document Assistance

A Legal Document Assistant is a registered professional who can prepare legal documents at your specific direction. LDAs are not attorneys and cannot provide legal advice, represent you in court, or interpret the law. We help you complete and file paperwork for self-represented (pro per) matters.

An attorney can provide legal advice, represent you in court, and make legal decisions on your behalf. An LDA can only prepare documents at your direction and provide general information. If your matter is contested or complex, you may need an attorney.

For contested matters (where the other party disagrees), we strongly recommend consulting with an attorney. We can only prepare documents for uncontested matters where both parties agree on terms.
